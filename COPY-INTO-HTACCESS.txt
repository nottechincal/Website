FAQ IS 404 BECAUSE YOUR .HTACCESS IS MISSING THESE LINES!

**STEP 1: Open cPanel File Manager**
Navigate to: /public_html/.htaccess

**STEP 2: Click "Edit"**

**STEP 3: Add These Lines (Copy & Paste EXACTLY)**

Find this section in your .htaccess:
```
# END WordPress
```

RIGHT AFTER that line, add these:

```apache
# FAQ and Booking Pages
RewriteRule ^faq/?$ /wp-content/themes/rapidtech-theme/faq.php [L]
RewriteRule ^book/?$ /wp-content/themes/rapidtech-theme/book.php [L]
```

**STEP 4: SAVE THE FILE**
