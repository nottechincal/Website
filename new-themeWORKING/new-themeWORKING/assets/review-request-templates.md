## WHEN TO SEND:

**Best Times:**
- 24-48 hours after service completion
- Morning (9-10am) or evening (7-8pm)
- Avoid weekends unless urgent

**Best Conditions:**
- Customer expressed satisfaction
- Problem was fully resolved
- You had positive interaction
- They mentioned recommending you

---

## GOOGLE REVIEW LINK SETUP:

1. Go to your Google Business Profile
2. Click "Share review form"
3. Copy the link
4. Use URL shortener (bit.ly) for SMS

Example: https://g.page/r/[YOUR-PROFILE-ID]/review

## HANDLING NEGATIVE FEEDBACK:

If customer expresses dissatisfaction:

1. **Don't ask for review** - Fix the problem first
2. **Apologise immediately** - "I'm sorry to hear that..."
3. **Offer solution** - "Let me make this right..."
4. **Follow up personally** - Call, don't email
5. **After resolution** - Then you can ask for review

Keep a simple spreadsheet:
